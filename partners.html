LEAD TIME

On average, we can process pallet orders in bulk pack within 5-7 days. Delivery of orders will ultimately depend on the packing requirement, order quantity and the schedule of our pending orders.

PAYMENT TERMS

  • For orders of 3 pallets and below, 100% advance payment is required 5 business days prior to delivery or pick up.
  • For orders upwards of 3 pallets, a 50% down-payment is required upon PO issuance, with the balance settled 5 business days prior to delivery.
  • Our preferred method of payment is Wire Transfer.
  • Prices are subject to change, kindly contact us for current pricing.

PORT OF SHIPMENT

We ship out from Davao, Philippines

EXPORT/IMPORT OPTIONS

Ex-Works (EXW)

EXW is the price of the goods packed and ready for shipping. If you have the capabilities to arrange for logistics from pick up at our warehouse to loading and exporting, then this is a good option for the buyer who wants more control over logistic costs.

Free on Board (FOB)

FOB price includes transportation of the finished goods to the port of shipment, and all loading and local charges. The buyer is responsible for freight, insurance, offloading and other logistic costs to final destination.

If you have a nominated Consolidator in the Philippines, we would be happy to coordinate with them on your behalf.

LICENSES AND DOCUMENTATION

Before contacting us, it would be helpful to be aware of the licenses or documents required by your local customs, to speed up transactions. Specification Sheets can be provided for you upon special request. Certain lab tests are available and may be readily shared with the buyer. Specialty tests may be ordered, depending on accessibility and on the account of the buyer.
