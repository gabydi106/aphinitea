Magnolia



In the Philippines, the coconut palm (cocos nucifera) is known as "the tree of life". Coming from the tree of a thousand uses, Coconut Sugar can also be used in a number of ways:

Cocoro Dark Caramel
  • A great substitute for brown sugar
  • Can be used for coffee, strong teas, chocolate and caramel recipes
  • Gives a darker finished product
  • More aroma, more bulking, not as sweet
Cocoro Ivory
  • Has a delicate flavor
  • Complements light citrus or berry recipes
  • Can also be used in vanilla custards, ice cream, and yogurts
  • does not darken the finished product as much
  • more sweetness
