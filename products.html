Cocoro Organic Coconut Sugar



In the Philippines, the coconut palm (cocos nucifera) is known as "the tree of life". Coming from the tree of a thousand uses, Coconut Sugar can also be used in a number of ways:

Cocoro Dark Caramel
  • A great substitute for brown sugar
  • Can be used for coffee, strong teas, chocolate and caramel recipes
  • Gives a darker finished product
  • More aroma, more bulking, not as sweet
Cocoro Ivory
  • Has a delicate flavor
  • Complements light citrus or berry recipes
  • Can also be used in vanilla custards, ice cream, and yogurts
  • does not darken the finished product as much
  • more sweetness

Avroko Peanut Spread

Made with Coconut Sugar



Avroko Crunchy is made with blended roasted peanuts, coconut sugar, sea salt and extra toasted nuts added in. It’s great for sandwiches or as a topping for smoothies, pancakes or ice cream.

Avroko Creamy is a smooth peanut butter made with roasted peanuts, coconut sugar and a touch of sea salt.

Avroko Skinny is a specially formulated peanut butter, with more peanut skin added to it. The delicate reddish skin of the peanut (not the shell!) is known to be a rich source of antioxidants and dietary fiber.

Avroko Dark Chocolate is made with tablea, an unsweetened, native Philippine chocolate. It has a rich cocoa flavour balanced with the aroma of cocoa butter. Blended with roasted peanuts, coconut sugar and sea salt, makes for a nutritious, protein-rich and delicious spread.
